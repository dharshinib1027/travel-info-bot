=== CHENNAI PLACES TO VISIT ===
Marina Beach - Anna Salai - World's second longest beach - 13 km long - Entry Free - Best time: Early morning or evening - Nearby: Lighthouse, Aquarium
Kapaleeshwarar Temple - Mylapore - Ancient Dravidian architecture - Entry Free - Timings: 5 AM to 12 PM and 4 PM to 9 PM - Famous for Arubathumoovar festival
Fort St. George - Near Rajaji Bhavan - Built in 1644 - First English fortress in India - Entry Rs.5 - Timings: 9 AM to 5 PM
Arignar Anna Zoological Park - Vandalur - One of largest zoos in Asia - Entry Rs.50 adults Rs.20 children - Timings: 9 AM to 5 PM - Closed Tuesday
Government Museum Chennai - Egmore - Bronze gallery, Natural history - Entry Rs.15 - Timings: 9:30 AM to 5 PM - Closed Friday
Santhome Cathedral - Mylapore - Built over tomb of St. Thomas - Entry Free - Timings: 6 AM to 8 PM
DakshinaChitra - ECR Road - Open air museum of South Indian culture - Entry Rs.150 - Timings: 10 AM to 6 PM - Closed Tuesday
Mahabalipuram - 55 km from Chennai - UNESCO World Heritage Shore Temple - Entry Rs.40 - Best day trip from Chennai
Crocodile Bank - 40 km from Chennai - Largest crocodile park - Entry Rs.50 - Timings: 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM

=== MUMBAI PLACES TO VISIT ===
Gateway of India - Colaba - Built in 1924 - Entry Free - Best time: Early morning - Nearby: Taj Mahal Palace Hotel, Ferry to Elephanta
Elephanta Caves - Ferry from Gateway of India - UNESCO World Heritage - Entry Rs.40 - Ferry Fare Rs.200 return - Timings: 9 AM to 5 PM - Closed Monday
Marine Drive - Nariman Point - 3.6 km promenade - Entry Free - Best time: Sunset - Known as Queen's Necklace at night
Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus - CST - UNESCO World Heritage Railway Station - Entry Free - Victorian Gothic architecture
Juhu Beach - Andheri West - Popular beach - Entry Free - Famous for street food: Pav Bhaji, Bhel Puri, Vada Pav
Siddhivinayak Temple - Prabhadevi - Famous Ganesh temple - Entry Free - Timings: 5:30 AM to 10 PM - Long queues on Tuesdays
Haji Ali Dargah - Worli - Iconic mosque on island - Entry Free - Timings: 5:30 AM to 10 PM - Accessible only during low tide
Bandra-Worli Sea Link - Bandra - 5.6 km cable-stayed bridge - Toll Rs.85
Chhota Kashmir - Aarey Colony - Mini Kashmir in Mumbai - Entry Free
Film City - Goregaon - Bollywood studio tours - Entry Rs.600 - Timings: 10 AM to 5 PM

=== DELHI PLACES TO VISIT ===
Red Fort - Chandni Chowk - Mughal masterpiece - UNESCO World Heritage - Entry Rs.35 Indians Rs.500 foreigners - Timings: Sunrise to Sunset - Closed Monday
Qutub Minar - Mehrauli - World's tallest brick minaret - UNESCO World Heritage - Entry Rs.40 - Timings: Sunrise to Sunset
India Gate - Rajpath - War memorial - Entry Free - Best time: Evening - Nearby: Rashtrapati Bhavan, Parliament
Humayun's Tomb - Nizamuddin - Mughal tomb - UNESCO World Heritage - Entry Rs.40 - Timings: Sunrise to Sunset
Lotus Temple - Bahapur - Bahai House of Worship - Entry Free - Timings: 9 AM to 5:30 PM - Closed Monday
Akshardham Temple - Noida More - Stunning temple complex - Entry Free for temple - Show tickets Rs.170 to Rs.280 - Timings: 10 AM to 6:30 PM - Closed Monday
Chandni Chowk - Old Delhi - Historic market - Entry Free - Famous for street food, spices, textiles
Lodi Garden - Lodi Road - Mughal era tombs in a garden - Entry Free - Timings: Sunrise to Sunset
Connaught Place - Central Delhi - Shopping and dining hub - Entry Free - Famous for underground Palika Bazaar

=== GOA PLACES TO VISIT ===
Baga Beach - North Goa - Popular beach with water sports - Entry Free - Water sports: Parasailing Rs.500, Jet ski Rs.500, Banana ride Rs.200
Calangute Beach - North Goa - Queen of Goa beaches - Entry Free - Best time: October to March
Anjuna Beach - North Goa - Famous flea market on Wednesdays - Entry Free - Market timings: 8 AM to Sunset
Basilica of Bom Jesus - Old Goa - UNESCO World Heritage - Contains remains of St. Francis Xavier - Entry Free - Timings: 9 AM to 6:30 PM
Se Cathedral - Old Goa - Largest church in Asia - Entry Free - Timings: 7:30 AM to 6:30 PM
Fort Aguada - Sinquerim - 17th century Portuguese fort - Entry Free - Timings: 9:30 AM to 6 PM - Lighthouse with views
Dudhsagar Falls - Mollem - Four-tiered waterfall - 310 meters high - Entry Rs.400 including jeep safari - Best time: Monsoon to January
Spice Plantation - Ponda - Tour through spice gardens - Entry Rs.400 including lunch - Timings: 9 AM to 4 PM
Palolem Beach - South Goa - Crescent shaped beach - Entry Free - Calm waters, ideal for swimming

=== OOTY PLACES TO VISIT ===
Ooty Lake - Ooty Town - Artificial lake built in 1824 - Boating Rs.50 per person - Timings: 9 AM to 6 PM
Botanical Garden - Ooty - 22 hectares of gardens - Entry Rs.30 adults Rs.15 children - Timings: 9 AM to 6:30 PM - 650 species of plants
Doddabetta Peak - 10 km from Ooty - Highest peak in Nilgiris 2637 meters - Entry Rs.10 - Telescope house available
Nilgiri Mountain Railway - UNESCO World Heritage - Toy train from Mettupalayam to Ooty - Tickets Rs.30 to Rs.215 - Book in advance
Rose Garden - Vijayanagaram - Largest rose garden in India - Over 20000 rose varieties - Entry Rs.15 - Timings: 9 AM to 6 PM
Mudumalai Wildlife Sanctuary - 67 km from Ooty - Elephants, tigers, leopards - Entry Rs.35 - Safari Rs.150 - Timings: 6 AM to 6 PM
Pykara Lake and Falls - 20 km from Ooty - Scenic lake and waterfalls - Boating available Rs.100 - Entry Free
Tea Museum - Ooty - Learn about tea processing - Entry Rs.35 - Timings: 9 AM to 5 PM - Closed Monday
Avalanche Lake - 28 km from Ooty - Pristine mountain lake - Entry Free - Trout fishing allowed
