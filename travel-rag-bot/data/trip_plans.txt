=== CHENNAI 3-DAY TRIP PLAN ===
Day 1 - Heritage and Beach:
Morning 7 AM: Start at Marina Beach - Walk along the shore, visit lighthouse
Morning 9 AM: Kapaleeshwarar Temple - Mylapore - Explore Dravidian architecture
Lunch 12 PM: Murugan Idli Shop - Mylapore - Try authentic South Indian breakfast
Afternoon 2 PM: Fort St. George and Government Museum - Egmore
Evening 5 PM: San Thome Cathedral - Mylapore
Dinner 7 PM: Ponnusamy Hotel - T Nagar - Try Chettinad chicken, Biryani
Budget for Day 1: Rs.500 to Rs.800 per person

Day 2 - Culture and Shopping:
Morning 9 AM: DakshinaChitra - 30 km from city - Traditional art and culture
Lunch 1 PM: Local restaurant near ECR - Try seafood
Afternoon 3 PM: T Nagar Shopping - Sarees, textiles, jewelry
Evening 6 PM: Express Avenue Mall - Royapettah - Shopping and food court
Dinner 8 PM: Buhari Hotel - Anna Salai - Famous for Chicken 65 and Biryani
Budget for Day 2: Rs.1000 to Rs.3000 per person including shopping

Day 3 - Day Trip to Mahabalipuram:
Morning 8 AM: Drive to Mahabalipuram - 55 km on ECR - 1.5 hours
Morning 10 AM: Shore Temple and Five Rathas - UNESCO Heritage site
Lunch 1 PM: Moonrakers Restaurant - Mahabalipuram - Seafood on the beach
Afternoon 3 PM: Arjuna's Penance, Tiger Cave exploration
Evening 5 PM: Return to Chennai
Dinner 8 PM: Hotel Saravana Bhavan - Try South Indian thali
Budget for Day 3: Rs.600 to Rs.1000 per person

Total 3 Day Budget Chennai: Rs.2000 to Rs.5000 per person excluding hotel
